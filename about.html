I am from Italy, a country famous for creativity and style. I appreciate beauty, quality, craftsmanship and I am committed to empowering women.

I was the lead stylist, buyer and visual merchandiser for a consultancy boutique in Mayfair for seven years. Since then, I have worked independently, styling over 200 successful women, from many different walks of life.

I have also worked with some of the biggest names in fashion and I collaborate with top fashion houses for London, Milan and Paris fashion week.

I advise in association with leading luxury brands, including Tom Ford and Alfred Dunhill. I also hold private events to showcase new and exciting collections and guide you to create your own personal style.
